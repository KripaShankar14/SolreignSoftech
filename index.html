FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have extensive experience across multiple industries including eCommerce, FinTech, Healthcare, Education, and SaaS. Our team adapts to your specific industry requirements to deliver tailored solutions that address your unique challenges.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Most web applications take 4-8 weeks, while mobile apps typically require 8-12 weeks. Complex enterprise solutions or AI projects may take 3-6 months. We provide detailed timelines after our initial discovery phase.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer flexible support and maintenance packages. Options include bug fixes, feature enhancements, performance optimization, and 24/7 monitoring. Many clients choose our retainer model for continuous improvement of their digital products.

What's your development methodology?

We follow Agile methodologies with 2-week sprints, daily standups, and regular demos. This ensures transparency, adaptability, and continuous delivery of value. Clients have access to project management tools to track progress in real-time.
